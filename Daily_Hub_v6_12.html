Formula (standard margin, per contract)
OTM % (put: strike below spot)
(Spot − Strike) ÷ Spot = OTM%
Margin factor
MAX(0.15 − OTM%, 0.10) + (Premium ÷ Spot)
Margin in USDC (per contract = 1 coin)
Margin factor × Spot × Contract size = USDC margin
